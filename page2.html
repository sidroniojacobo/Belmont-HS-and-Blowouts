Project's Purpose


The purpose of this project is to shed light on the involvement of students, families, and community partners in rallying support at Belmont High School, one of central high schools of the Chicanx Blowouts 1968. In the evolving scholarship on the Chicanx Blowouts and the larger Chicanx movements, Belmont High School is often briefly mention, if even mentioned. Belmont High School merits attention because unlike the other major high schools that participated in the Blowouts, it was not in East Los Angeles, it did not serve a majority Mexican-American student population, and most importantly was one of the only Los Angeles high schools serving non-US citizen students.

What are some key distinct events that unraveled at Belmont High School that may have affected the protest and walkouts often associated with the Chicano Blowouts of 1968?
