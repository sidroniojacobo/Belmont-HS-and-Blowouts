About the Author

Sidronio Jacobo

Sidronio Jacobo is a fourth year PhD student in the Higher Education and Organizational Change program at UCLA's School of Education and Information Science. He also holds a Bachelor's Degree from UC Berkeley and a Master's Degree from UCLA. He was inspired to do this project on Belmont High School, student organizing, and social movements from his experiences in high school and undergrad. When I was in high school, I was able to attend the Chicano Youth Leadership Conference in Malibu and learned about the legacies of the Chicano Blowouts and how Belmont HS was involved. While at UC Berkeley, I continued to be involved in student organizing. When I came to UCLA, I knew I wanted to use the resources here to learn and teach others about the Chicano Blowouts.
